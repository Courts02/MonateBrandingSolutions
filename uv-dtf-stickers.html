What Is UV-DTF?

Professional UV-DTF Sticker Services

UV-DTF (Ultraviolet Direct-to-Film) stickers are a game-changing way to brand hard surfaces with vibrant, full-colour graphics — no heat press or special equipment needed. Just peel and stick for instant impact.

Key Features

  • High-resolution UV prints with stunning clarity
  • Strong adhesive that bonds to glass, plastic, metal, wood, and more
  • Scratch-resistant and fade-proof for long-lasting results
  • No bubbling or wrinkling — smooth, professional finish
  • Custom shapes and sizes available for branding or crafting

Perfect for mugs, tumblers, phone cases, packaging, and promo items — Monate’s UV-DTF range turns everyday objects into branded masterpieces.

Our UV-DTF Sticker Products

Our UV-DTF Stickers Premium, peel-and-stick decals for hard surfaces. No heat press required. Waterproof, scratch-resistant, and perfect for high-impact branding.

Where You Can Stick 'Em:

  • Glass & Mirrors: Drinkware, décor, signage
  • Plastic: Keychains, containers, phone cases
  • Metal: Stainless steel, aluminum, branded tools
  • Wood: MDF, plywood, gift boxes, signage
  • Acrylic: Displays, keychains, nameplates
  • Ceramic: Mugs, tiles, coasters
  • Leather & Faux Leather: Wallets, belts, accessories
  • PVC & Vinyl: Labels, decals, custom packaging
  • Canvas & Hardboard: Art prints, signage
  • Stone & Cork: Coasters, decorative pieces
  • Curved Surfaces: Bottles, pens, tumblers
