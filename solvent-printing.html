What Is Solvent Printing?

Professional Solvent Printing Services

What Is Solvent Printing? Solvent printing uses durable, fade-resistant inks that are chemically based (solvent or eco-solvent) to bond directly onto non-absorbent surfaces. It’s ideal for outdoor signage, vehicle branding, and long-lasting graphics because the ink penetrates the material and resists water, UV rays, and scratches. Whether you're wrapping a bakkie, branding a shopfront, or creating custom décor, solvent printing delivers vibrant, weatherproof results that stick.

Materials Printed On Using Solvent Ink:

  • Vinyl – Self-adhesive, weatherproof, perfect for vehicles, windows, and decals.
  • Contra Vision – Perforated one-way film for shopfronts and car windows.
  • PVC Banner – Durable, flexible vinyl for outdoor signage and event branding.
  • Canvas – Textured, high-quality prints for photos, art, and premium décor.
  • Wallpaper – Custom wall coverings for branded interiors or creative spaces.
